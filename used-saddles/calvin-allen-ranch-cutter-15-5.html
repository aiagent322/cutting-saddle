Calvin Allen Saddlery out of Weatherford, Texas built saddles for riders who value function over flash. This Ranch Cutter is exactly that — full basketweave tooling across every panel, a braided rawhide horn wrap that's ready to grab, antiqued berry conchos, and woolskin lining with even contact wear that tells you this saddle was fitted and used correctly. The stamp reads clearly: Custom Made / Calvin Allen / Weatherford, TX.

At 15½ inches this is a compact cutting seat that suits a wide range of riders who prefer to sit in the saddle rather than on top of it. Weatherford is the center of the cutting horse world — a Calvin Allen saddle built there carries provenance. This is a working-competitor's buy at an honest price for a rider who needs a solid no-frills cutting platform. Contact David to discuss tree width for your horse.
